Quick Quote Calculator case study images
=========================================

Save the screenshots into THIS folder using these exact filenames.
They are referenced by src/pages/QuickQuote.jsx. Until a file exists,
the page shows a labeled placeholder in its spot (so nothing breaks).

  calculator.png  -> the calculator form (garment, quantity, locations, colors)
  quote.png       -> the instant price estimate / result the customer sees
  sheet.png       -> the linked Google Sheet where the shop edits its pricing

PNG or JPG both work, but keep the filename + .png extension as written
(or update the extensions in src/pages/QuickQuote.jsx to match).
Anything in /public is served from the site root, so these load at
/case-studies/quick-quote/<filename> with no import needed.

You can also upload these from the dashboard (/admin → Case Studies →
Quick Quote) instead of dropping files here — uploaded images win.
